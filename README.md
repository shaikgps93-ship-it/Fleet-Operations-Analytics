# 🚖 Fleet Operations Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI project focused on analyzing Bolt & Uber fleet operations for May 2026. This project transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and data storytelling.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

To analyze driver performance, trip activity, productivity, and vehicle utilization in order to identify operational trends, inefficiencies, and opportunities for improvement.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

- Data Modeling

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Top-performing drivers earned over €10K during May 2026.
- Driver availability strongly influenced completed rides.
- Peak demand occurred during evening hours (18:00–20:00).
- The fleet covered more than 411K km and generated over €815K in revenue.
- A small number of vehicles contributed a significant share of total revenue.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Optimize trip allocation across vehicles and drivers.
- Monitor high-mileage assets through preventive maintenance.
- Reward consistently high-performing drivers.
- Improve utilization of underperforming vehicles.
- Use analytics-driven decision making to improve fleet efficiency.
